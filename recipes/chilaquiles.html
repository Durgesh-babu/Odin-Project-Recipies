Chilaquiles

Chilaquiles are basically corn tortilla pieces that are fried, cooked in salsa, and sprinkled with cheese.
They are often served for breakfast with eggs and a side of beans or nopalitos.

Ingredients:

  • 8 (6 inch) corn tortillas, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 5 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 8 eggs, beaten
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Â½ cup green taco sauce
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Instructions to prepare:

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add tortilla pieces to oil and fry until crisp, about 5 minutes.
  3. Pour beaten eggs over the tortilla pieces in the skillet.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook, stirring occasionally, until eggs have set, about 4 minutes.
